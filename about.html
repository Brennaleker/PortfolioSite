About me

I love learning, creating things, sharing ideas, letting my nerd flag fly and tinkering around on computers. This has translated into a fascination with web development.

It all started with a baking blog. I had a clear vision of what I wanted but I could not find any free WordPress templates that matched my needs. I taught myself a bit of code and used my spring break – I was a public school teacher at the time – to hack away at the Twenty Ten theme source code until I had something I was happy with.

Flash forward a few years and I found myself once again learning to code for fun; losing track of time on Treehouse, Codecademy and tinkering on my own projects. I decided it was time to get serious and took a few courses through Peninsula Community College.

I am a front-end web developer with a passion for making the web a better place, a fascination with JS and an excitement for language.

I am currently working on personal projects, hunting for a position doing what I love and continuing to delve deeper into the intricacies of CSS and JavaScript.
